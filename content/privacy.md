## Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to me. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled on **hemendra.in**, a personal website owned and operated from **India**, and published under the **.in** domain.

This policy is intended to comply with applicable Indian laws, including the **Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act)**.

By accessing or using this website, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

## Nature of This Website

This is a **static personal website** built using **Hugo** and the **PaperMod** theme, created to share technical content, blogs, and project information.

- The site does **not** provide contact forms, comment sections, subscriptions, or user accounts.
- No personal data is directly collected, requested, or stored by this website.
- Any communication with the site owner occurs externally via third-party platforms.

---

## Information We Collect

This website does **not** knowingly collect **personal data** as defined under the DPDP Act.

However, limited **non-personal and aggregated usage information** may be collected automatically through analytics services, such as:

- Pages visited and navigation patterns  
- Browser and device type  
- Approximate geographic region  
- Session duration  

This information is used solely for understanding website usage and improving content quality.

## Google Analytics

This website uses **Google Analytics** to analyze website traffic and improve user experience. The analytics configuration follows privacy-respecting options provided by Hugo, including:

- **Respect for “Do Not Track” (DNT):**  
  Analytics tracking is disabled for users who enable DNT in their browser.
- **Session-based storage:**  
  Session storage is used instead of persistent cookies where supported.
- **No intentional identification:**  
  The site owner does not attempt to identify individual users.

Google may process aggregated usage data on servers located outside India in accordance with its own privacy policies and applicable laws.

## Consent Under Indian Law (DPDP Act)

Under the DPDP Act, users are referred to as **Data Principals**, and the website owner acts as the **Data Fiduciary**.

By continuing to browse this website, you provide **voluntary, informed, and specific consent** for the limited processing of non-personal and aggregated usage data as described in this Privacy Policy.

### Managing Analytics Preferences

You may limit or opt out of analytics collection by:

- Enabling “Do Not Track” in your browser  
- Adjusting browser cookie and storage settings  
- Using Google’s Analytics opt-out tool:  
  https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout  

Disabling analytics does not affect access to website content.
